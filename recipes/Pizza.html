Pizza

Description

Pizza is a dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened
wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients
(such as anchovies, mushrooms, onions, olives, vegetables, meat, etc.), which is then baked
at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Pizza sauce
  • Pepperoni
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450ÂºF.
  2. Roll/stretch out the dough on a clean and well floured surface. If dough starts to get a little sticky sprinkle more flour.
  3. Using the back of a spoon spread the sauce evenly over the center of the pizza, leaving just a little crust space. Don't over sauce your pizza or it might get soggy, you may not use all your pizza sauce.
  4. Add the cheese in a single layer covering the sauce.
  5. Add all your favorite toppings, but be cautious not to add too much so that it gets too heavy.
  6. Cook 10-12 minutes until the cheese on the pizza is melted and starting to turn golden brown.
  7. Carefully remove from oven. Transfer to a cutting board, let rest for at least one minute then cut it and enjoy! Repeat process for more pizzas!
